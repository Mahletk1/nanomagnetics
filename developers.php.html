Nano Magnetics Developer Program

The Nano Developer Program offers resources, support and materials for developing consumer magnet products including the Nanoport™. Developers have access to pre-release products, prototypes and specifications of the latest magnetic mechanisms for developing new innovative products, and research & development.

Hardware Development Kit

This program is intended for hardware developers who wish to develop Nanoport enabled electronic accessories.

  • Hardware connectors and components
  • Testing tools
  • Technical information
  • Technical support
  • Product certification
  • Nanoport logos and icons

Software Development Program

For a developer who wants to create applications for Nanoport enabled devices.

  • High Powered Magnet Development
  • Structure and magnetism
  • Exploring the interaction of magnetism and kinetic energy
  • The study of magnets and motion
  • Advanced applications (space, military, government)
