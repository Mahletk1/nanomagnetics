Magnet Safety

At Nano Magnetics, we take safety seriously. Safety is a primary component of our entire product development lifecycle, from conception through finished product.

Our products are designed, manufactured, and tested by third-party laboratories to conform to stringent internal safety standards, government regulations, and independent standards.

We appreciate your interest in product safety and encourage you to contact us at the Nano Magnetics Consumer Hotline at 1-888-629-NANO with your safety questions, comments, and concerns.
