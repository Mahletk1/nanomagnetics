Corporate Information

Nano Magnetics is a product innovation company founded in 2009 and based in Toronto, Canada. Nano specializes in magnet technologies, new product development, and worldwide commercialization under in-house brands, through a distribution network spanning 15+ countries. Famous for the NanodotsÂ® Magnetic Constructors, Nano is the largest manufacturer of magnetic spheres in the world.

Over the years, Nano Magnetics received funding from different financial corporation and entities, including Fidelity, BlackRock, and AdStage.

Today Nano continues to grow its portfolio of retail products and intellectual property in the magnet space, taking a leadership role in the magnet industry through ASTM and International Consumer Magnets Association (ICMA).
