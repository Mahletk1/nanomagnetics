Retailers

Our retailer value proposition is tailored to the needs and goals of large retail chains as well as small specialty stores. Nano Magnetics is committed to creating a strong lasting relationship with each and every retail partner by providing excellent customer service, fast turnaround, and comprehensive marketing support.

Our FREE reseller platform provides for convenient account management from anywhere anytime, including order placement, tracking, account history, updates, and multiple location orders. Our secure login will provide access to real-time pricing and delivery estimates, and serves as a direct gateway to the sales and customer service departments.

To learn more about vendor opportunities with Nano Magnetics, please complete an email form or contact us toll-free by calling 1-888-629-NANO.
