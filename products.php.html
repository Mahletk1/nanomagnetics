Nanodots® & Accessories

Nanodots are magnet spheres precision-milled from sintered Neodymium-Iron-Boron. Measuring 5mm in diameter, Nanodots are designed to model atomic interactions at a human scale. Optimized for maximum structural integrity, Nanodots were used to set a Guinness World Record for the largest magnet sculpture in 2011 in Los Angeles. The record remains unchallenged at 550,000 Nanodots pieces weighing 600 lbs. Nano carries a series of accessories for enthusiasts, including a Nanopad, magnetic surface and gaming board, and a series of Splitter cards, a tool for exploring the nature of Nanodots and associated magnetism.

nanodots.com

Dotpedia.com

Dotpedia is the web's encyclopedia for magnet discoveries, art and sculpture. It is home to thousands of pictures and videos of user-submitted creations from around the world. Follow top builders, submit your own creations and learn from the best. The Dotpedia community platform was built from the ground up by Nano Magnetics.

MEGA Nanodots

MEGA Nanodots were developed for exploring the interaction of magnetism and kinetic energy. Made with a special formulation of military-grade ceramic ferrite, MEGA Nanodots transfer kinetic energy through touching masses similar to a Newton's cradle. They can also be used for assembling basic structures similar to the original magnetic constructors.
